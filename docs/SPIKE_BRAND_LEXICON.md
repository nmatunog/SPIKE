# SPIKE Brand Language & Lexicon v1.0

**Strategic Program for Internship Knowledge & Entrepreneurship (SPIKE)**

- **Version:** 1.0 (Working Draft)
- **Status:** Foundational Brand Document
- **Purpose:** Establish a consistent language across the SPIKE platform, UI/UX, presentations, mentor guides, documentation, AI Coach, and marketing materials.

---

## 1. Philosophy

SPIKE is not simply an internship.

SPIKE is a venture-building practicum where university interns develop the mindset, competencies, and experience of real financial entrepreneurs through AI, mentorship, collaboration, and real business execution.

The language of SPIKE should therefore reflect four principles:

- **Professional**
- **Aspirational**
- **Entrepreneurial**
- **Human-centered**

Avoid language that feels overly academic, corporate, or military while still maintaining credibility with universities and industry partners.

## 2. Brand Positioning

### What SPIKE is

- An AI-enabled Venture Practicum.
- A Financial Entrepreneurship Incubator.
- A bridge between university learning and real-world entrepreneurship.

### What SPIKE is NOT

- OJT Portal
- LMS
- Training Seminar
- Classroom
- Bootcamp
- Internship Tracker

SPIKE is a **venture-building ecosystem**.

## 3. Brand Identity

SPIKE is built around one central idea:

**Build your future by building a real venture.**

Every interaction should reinforce this message.

## 4. The SPIKE Journey

University → SPIKE Venture Practicum → Intern-Cadet → Associate Advisor → Financial Entrepreneur → Agency Leader → Mentor → Program Coach

*Future stages may evolve.*

## 5. SPIKE Roles

### Intern-Cadet (Current Working Title)

The official participant throughout SPIKE.

**Meaning:** University Intern · Entrepreneur-in-training · Future Financial Entrepreneur

The term intentionally combines the legitimacy of an internship with the identity of professional development.

**Used in:** Portal · Presentations · Program Coach Manual · Certificates · Portfolio · AI Coach

### Squad

Small peer learning team.

**Purpose:** accountability · collaboration · brainstorming · venture building · peer coaching

**Never use:** Group · Team · Class

### Mentor

Assigned coach of a Squad.

**Responsibilities:** weekly coaching · accountability · venture feedback · personal development · business discussions

Mentors coach. They don't lecture.

### Program Coach (Current Working Title)

Formerly Faculty.

**Responsibilities:** facilitates workshops · delivers content · evaluates outputs · oversees all squads · develops mentors · ensures learning quality

### Agency Leader

Experienced AIA leaders who share real-world experience.

**Examples:** Agency Directors · Unit Managers · MDRTs · Executive Advisors

### Industry Partner

External speakers · business owners · entrepreneurs · professionals.

*Future enhancement: Venture Partner*

## 10. AI Language

Never describe AI as replacing thinking.

SPIKE AI: challenges · refines · compares · suggests · coaches · expands ideas

**Never:** AI writes your answers.

**Instead:** AI helps you think better.

**Official description — AI Venture Coach:** Your AI thinking partner that helps refine—not replace—your ideas.

## 16. SPIKE Design Language

**Evoke:** Apple Keynotes · Google Workspace · Notion · Canva · Linear · IDEO · Y Combinator

**Not:** PowerPoint · Learning Management Systems · Corporate HR Training · Traditional Classrooms

## 17. SPIKE Tone of Voice

Confident · Modern · Encouraging · Human · Professional · Curious · Optimistic · Builder-oriented

Never arrogant. Never overly corporate. Never childish. Never overly academic.

## 18. SPIKE Design System Alignment

**Deck 1 — Inspire:** Cinematic · Premium · Documentary · Dark · Aspirational — Build trust, create excitement, inspire.

**Deck 2 — Build:** Bright · Open · Collaborative · Human-centered · Minimalist · Venture Studio — Transition participants into creation.

SPIKE should eventually become recognizable not only by its platform or curriculum, but by its language. Participants should naturally say things like:

- "Our Squad is presenting at the Venture Showcase."
- "My Mentor challenged my Venture Blueprint."
- "The AI Coach helped me refine my mission."
- "I just completed today's Build Challenge."
- "Our Program Coach reviewed our Financial Entrepreneurship Canvas."
