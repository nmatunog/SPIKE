SPIKE LIFE™
Simulation Blueprint v2.5
Life Stages, Progression Loops & Educational Journey Mapping
Version: 2.5
Purpose:
Define the progression system that guides players through increasingly complex financial situations while reinforcing SPIKE learning objectives.
This blueprint ensures SPIKE LIFE remains:
* A financial planning simulator
* An advisory thinking simulator
* A learning experience
and does not drift into becoming merely a personal finance game.

1. Core Progression Philosophy
SPIKE LIFE is built around one fundamental principle:
As life becomes more complex, financial decisions become more important.
The simulation should mirror reality.
Early life focuses on:
Income
Expenses
Habits

Middle life focuses on:
Family
Protection
Goals

Later life focuses on:
Wealth
Retirement
Legacy

The player's learning journey follows the same progression.

2. Educational Progression Model
SPIKE LIFE teaches players to answer five increasingly sophisticated questions.

Stage 1
Can I manage money?

Stage 2
Can I protect what matters?

Stage 3
Can I achieve major goals?

Stage 4
Can I build lasting wealth?

Stage 5
Can I create financial security beyond myself?

16. Difficulty Progression
The simulation should become more challenging over time.
Not because events become harsher.
But because responsibilities increase.

Example
Age 25
Critical Illness affects one person.

Age 40
Critical Illness affects:
Spouse
Children
Mortgage
Education Goals
Retirement Plan

The same event has larger consequences.

17. Success Framework
A successful player does NOT necessarily have the highest net worth.
A successful player:
Maintains positive cash flow
Protects dependents
Achieves meaningful goals
Builds sustainable wealth
Prepares retirement
Leaves a legacy

19. Design Guardrail
Any future feature, event, mechanic, UI element, or game mode must support at least one of the following:
Cash Flow Literacy
Protection Planning
Financial Needs Analysis
Goal Planning
Retirement Readiness
Advisory Thinking
Financial Entrepreneurship
If it does not support one of these objectives, it should not be included in SPIKE LIFE.
