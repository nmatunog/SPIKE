SPIKE LIFE™
Simulation Blueprint v2.6
Scoring, Feedback & Reflection System
Version: 2.6
Purpose:
Define how SPIKE LIFE measures progress, evaluates decisions, provides feedback, and transforms gameplay into learning outcomes.
This blueprint ensures the simulation remains an educational platform rather than simply a financial game.
The scoring system must reward:
* Sound financial decision-making
* Long-term thinking
* Financial resilience
* Protection readiness
* Goal achievement
* Advisory thinking
The scoring system must NOT reward:
* Pure risk-taking
* Pure net worth accumulation
* Luck
* Short-term optimization

1. Core Philosophy
SPIKE LIFE does not ask:
"How rich did you become?"
SPIKE LIFE asks:
"How well did you manage your financial life?"
The highest scorer should not necessarily be the wealthiest player.
The highest scorer should be the most financially prepared, balanced, and resilient.

24. Success Definition
A successful player:
Maintains positive cash flow
Protects dependents
Achieves meaningful goals
Builds sustainable wealth
Prepares retirement
Leaves a positive impact

A successful SPIKE LIFE player is not merely wealthy.
A successful SPIKE LIFE player is financially prepared, financially resilient, and capable of making sound planning decisions.
