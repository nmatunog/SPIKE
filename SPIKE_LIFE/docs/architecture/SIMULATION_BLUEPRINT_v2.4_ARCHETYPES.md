SPIKE LIFE™
Simulation Blueprint v2.4
Character Archetypes, Career Paths & Starting Scenarios
Version: 2.4
Purpose:
Define the playable starting personas for SPIKE LIFE.
Character archetypes represent realistic financial starting points that influence:
* Income
* Expenses
* Goals
* Risk Exposure
* Protection Needs
* FNA Priorities
* Learning Outcomes
The purpose is not role-playing.
The purpose is to experience different financial realities.

Design Principles
Every archetype must:
Represent a real-world financial profile.
Create meaningful planning decisions.
Expose different financial risks.
Demonstrate different FNA needs.
Support SPIKE learning objectives.

MVP Rule
Every SPIKE participant should be able to find a character that resembles their current life situation.
Every archetype must naturally expose at least one significant FNA gap.
The objective is not to create winners and losers.
The objective is to create teachable planning situations that develop financial decision-making and advisory thinking.
